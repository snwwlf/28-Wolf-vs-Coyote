About The Game

How to Play

To begin gameplay, set the Evasive Number by clicking the respective button that picks a number between 1 and 6. This represents the number the Coyote player will use to protect itself from receiving damage. For example, the evasive number is set to 3, meaning whenever the coyote rolls a 3, regardless of what the Wolf rolls, they will never lose points. This is known as evading an attack.

Each player, the Wolf and Coyote, start the game with 20 health points, which are displayed under their respective images. Throughout the game, players will lose points until a player reaches 0. Whichever player with points remaining wins the game.

Once the evasive button is set, gameplay will begin by clicking the Roll Dice button. This will display updated score, what number each player rolled, and a corresponding message below as to what occurred during that round of gameplay.

Round Outcomes

Wolf Critical Hit

When the Wolf player rolls a 6, they deal a critical hit upon the Coyote player where they lose 2 points instead of 1.

Evasive Roll

If the Coyote player rolls a number that matches their set Evasive number, for example, a 4, whenever they roll a 4, neither player loses points as Coyote has evaded an attack by the Wolf.

Coyote High-Score Evasive

If the Evasive number is set to 6, whenever the coyote player rolls a 6, the wolf player loses 1 point.

Standard Win

The Wolf and Coyote players each roll a die with numbers 1 through 6. Whoever rolls the higher number, with 6 being the highest one can roll, they will win that round of gameplay. The winning player will keep their current score while the losing player subtracts one point.

Tie

Should each player roll their respective die and have the same number, neither player loses points.

Winning The Game

Whichever player has points remaining after numerous rounds of gameplay, having not reached zero, is declared the winner. There is a respective banner displayed declaring the winner outright.

Game Reset

When the game ends once a player is declared the winner, or if the players wish to restart, they can click the Reset button to start a new game.
